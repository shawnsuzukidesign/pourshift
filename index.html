FAQ

The basics

What is PourShift? ▾
PourShift is a connection platform that helps Bay Area bars, restaurants, hotels, and breweries discover and hire verified bartenders and barbacks directly. Watch short pour videos, see live certification status, and skip the staffing agency. One flat fee per actual hire, no markup, no middleman.
Who is PourShift for? ▾
Two sides of the same room. Venues: Bay Area bars, restaurants, hotels, breweries, and event spaces hiring bartenders or barbacks. Workers: certified bartenders, mixologists, and barbacks (21+, legally authorized to work in the US) looking to be discovered for the skill they actually have, not the resume they spent hours writing.
Is PourShift a staffing agency? ▾
No. PourShift is a connection platform that helps venues discover bartenders and barbacks. We are not a staffing agency, recruiter, or employer of record. Any hiring relationship is between the venue and the worker.
How is PourShift different from a staffing agency? ▾
Staffing agencies typically charge 20% to 30% of a worker's first-year compensation (often $10,000 or more per hire) and stay in the middle of the relationship. PourShift charges one flat fee on an actual hire and steps out. Venues hire workers directly on their own terms, with no markup, no ongoing fees, and no agency in the middle.
Does PourShift employ workers? ▾
No. PourShift does not employ, pay, manage, or schedule workers. The venue makes the hire and decides the terms.
How does the venue classify the worker? ▾
That's between the venue and the worker. PourShift doesn't dictate or get involved in employment classification, wages, taxes, or scheduling.
Is PourShift live yet? ▾
We're launching across the San Francisco Bay Area, with early access opening in waves by neighborhood. Join the waitlist and we'll email you when access opens in your area.

For venues

Why should a venue sign up for PourShift? ▾
You see how someone makes a drink before you ever schedule an interview. You see live certification status, so you don't waste a trail shift on a candidate whose RBS expired three months ago. You pay one flat fee on an actual hire instead of an agency markup or a job board you're paying for whether or not you find someone.
What does it cost? ▾
Free for workers, always. Venues pay $50 per invite and $200 total per hire ($150 due at hire after the invite fee is credited). Optional venue subscriptions: Standard ($20/mo) includes 3 invites a month, Pro ($50/mo) includes 10 invites, custom pour video requests, and shortlists.
Who can see my job opening? ▾
Job openings are visible to the public. Anonymous visitors see openings sorted oldest first, while signed-in bartenders see the newest openings first. Posting a job opening requires a Standard or Pro venue subscription.
Can I contact a worker before paying the invite fee? ▾
No. Last name and contact info are revealed only after the invite fee is paid. This keeps workers from being spammed and ensures venues are serious about hiring.
What happens if a hire doesn't work out? ▾
PourShift facilitates the connection. We don't guarantee fit. You're responsible for interviewing, verifying qualifications, and managing the working relationship. If the hire doesn't work out, you can invite another candidate and the cycle starts again. Hire fees are non-refundable.

For bartenders, mixologists, and barbacks

Why should a bartender, mixologist, or barback sign up? ▾
Get discovered for what you actually do behind the bar, not what you can fit on a resume. Build a profile once, record a set of skill videos, and let venues come to you. It's free to join, free to use, and you earn a $75 bonus on each of your first 4 hires through the platform, paid by ACH or gift card.
How many videos do I get? ▾
Around 5 to 10 short videos that show your range. You record a single take while you take an order, make the drink, and present it. We cut the footage into sections for you. The point isn't to nail a perfect pour for the camera, it's to show how you actually work behind the bar. You select one as your feature video, which is visible to all signed-in venues for free. The rest live behind a paywall for paying venues.
Who can see my profile? ▾
Only signed-in venues can view bartender profiles. Free venues see a basic card with your first name, city, signature drink, pour time, cert status, and time commitment, plus your feature video and thumbnails of your other videos. Your full video library and your last name and contact info are revealed only after a venue pays to unlock or sends an interview invite.
What's the cert-priority badge? ▾
Optional $9.99/mo subscription for bartenders. Adds a green check badge to your profile and boosts you above non-subscribers in venue search results when your certification is current and your profile is complete. Cancel anytime.
What certifications do you track? ▾
California RBS, food handler, and city-specific permits to start. Early access members help shape which additional certs we add.
What happens if my certifications expire? ▾
You'll be reclassified as a barback and can only apply to barback positions until you renew. We send reminders before expiration so you can renew in time.
I got hired, what do I do next? ▾
Congrats. Once the venue confirms the hire and pays the remaining hire fee, your $75 bonus (if eligible) is paid out within 7 to 10 business days. You'll choose ACH transfer or a gift card when you set up your payout method. From there, your working relationship with the venue is between you and them. We're here if you need us.
